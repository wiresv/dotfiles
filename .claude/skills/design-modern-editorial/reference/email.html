Letter № xiv · 28 April 2026 · spring list

A short note on a quiet Tuesday.

Dear friend —

A quick note from the desk this morning. Three manuscripts cleared copy overnight, two more were sent to the typesetter, and the warehouse in Rhinebeck dispatched nine hundred and twelve advance copies of The Carpathian Letters1 before noon. A productive day, and a small one.

In this letter

  • i.The Carpathian Letters, in the world
  • ii.A note on Pevsner's Bestiary
  • iii.Beasley's reading at McNally's, late May

Pevsner's An Incomplete Bestiary2 came back from the typesetter looking remarkable — one running-head correction on the verso, otherwise pristine. We expect to be at press by the end of next week, and bound copies in early July.

Diane Beasley has confirmed Friday, 22 May, for her launch reading at McNally's in Brooklyn. You'll have an invitation in a separate note.

1The Carpathian Letters — Anya Voronova, trans. P. Hale. ISBN 978-1-948-21102-6. Available at independent booksellers from today.

2An Incomplete Bestiary — Margaret Pevsner. Forthcoming July 2026; pre-orders open in May.

Yours in the work,
M. Aguila — for the desk
