i.

Local-first, not cloud-only

Your notes are files on your disk — readable in any text editor, portable to any machine, never held hostage by a subscription.

ii.

An archival file format

Plain markdown with a small, documented frontmatter. The format will be readable, with or without us, in fifty years.

iii.

Quietly indexed

Backlinks, dates, tags — all derived from your prose. Nothing to configure. Nothing to migrate. Just write.
